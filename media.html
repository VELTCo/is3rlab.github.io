Research Presentation


A Hierarchical Game-Theoretic Decision-Making for Cooperative Multiagent Systems Under the Presence of Adversarial Agents

The relationships among Multi-agent systems (MAS) working in hazardous and risky scenarios are complex, and game theory provides various methods to model agents' behaviors and strategies. This research proposes a new network model called the Game-Theoretic Utility Tree (GUT), combining the core principles of game theory and utility theory to achieve cooperative decision-making for MAS in adversarial environments. [SAC Paper | 03/28/2023]
